Miscellaneous

In this video, the creator, UrAvgConsumer spends a week fully switching to Marques Brownlee's (MKBHD's) tech setup from his desk layout, all the way to his accessories and workflow to see if it improves daily productivity and comfort. He tests things phones, laptops, chargets, headphones, backpack, etc. noting what feels more efficient versus what's overkill. By the end, they break down which parts of MKBHD's setup genuinely enhanced their workflow (like certain peripherals or layout choices) and which didn't suit their personal needs, concluding that while people have different needs, and carry what caters their needs best. There is no one size fits all.
